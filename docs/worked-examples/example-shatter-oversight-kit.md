# Worked Example: Shatter + Oversight Kit Framework

This example demonstrates combining the Shatter Protocol with the Oversight & Verification Kit Framework.

## Query
"Develop an oversight approach for examining a nonprofit organization’s influence on federal education policy, including funding flows and narrative coordination."

## Step 1: Apply Shatter Protocol
Shatter. Shatter.

Key Revelations:
- The public narrative frames the organization’s role as purely expertise-driven and philanthropic.
- There are patterns of coordinated messaging, personnel overlap with government roles, and selective disclosure of funding sources.
- The “independent expert” framing may obscure coordinated influence efforts.

## Step 2: Apply Oversight Kit Framework
Using the structured oversight approach:

**Scope Definition:** Nonprofit’s influence on federal education policy over the past 7 years, with focus on funding, personnel, and narrative alignment.

**Evidence Collection Areas:**
- Funding sources and grant patterns
- Personnel movement between the organization and government agencies
- Public statements, reports, and media coverage alignment
- Disclosure practices around conflicts of interest

**Key Findings & Red Flags:**
- Multiple instances of personnel moving between the nonprofit and federal roles
- High alignment in policy framing between the organization’s outputs and certain agency communications
- Incomplete or delayed disclosure of certain funding relationships

**Output Structure:**
- Executive Summary
- Funding & Personnel Mapping
- Narrative Alignment Analysis
- Recommendations for Further Oversight

## Final Synthesized Output
The organization exerts measurable influence on federal education policy through funding relationships, personnel placement, and narrative coordination. While some activities are disclosed, the full scope of coordination and influence is not transparently presented to the public. A structured oversight approach reveals patterns that warrant deeper examination.

Key Takeaway:
Shatter helped surface the gap between the public narrative and observable patterns. The Oversight Kit Framework then provided a repeatable structure for building a verifiable oversight package.
