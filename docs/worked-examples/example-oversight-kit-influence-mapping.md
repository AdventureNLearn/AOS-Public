# Worked Example: Oversight Kit + Influence Mapping

This example combines the Oversight Kit Framework with Influence Mapping for a comprehensive oversight project.

## Query
"Design an oversight package to examine a coalition of organizations pushing for changes to state healthcare regulations, including funding, personnel, and narrative alignment."

## Step 1: Apply Influence Mapping Framework
Mapped the coalition’s key players, funding sources, personnel overlaps, and messaging patterns over a 5-year period.

**Key Findings:**
- Concentrated funding from a small number of national foundations
- Multiple instances of personnel moving between coalition members and state agencies
- High alignment in public framing and report timing across organizations

## Step 2: Apply Oversight Kit Framework
Built a structured oversight package:

**Scope:** Coalition influence on state healthcare regulation changes (2018–2025).

**Evidence Collection:**
- Grant and tax filing data
- Personnel movement records
- Public reports, press releases, and media coverage
- Regulatory comment records

**Findings & Red Flags:**
- Significant personnel overlap between coalition organizations and regulatory bodies
- Coordinated release of reports timed with regulatory comment periods
- Incomplete disclosure of certain funding relationships in public materials

**Output Package:**
- Executive Summary
- Influence Map (visual + data)
- Narrative Alignment Analysis
- Recommendations for Further Oversight or Disclosure Requirements

## Final Synthesized Output
A relatively small network of organizations and funders has exerted considerable influence over the direction of state healthcare regulation debates. While some activities are visible, the full extent of coordination and personnel overlap is not transparently disclosed. A combined Influence Mapping + Oversight Kit approach produces a clear, evidence-based package suitable for public or legislative review.

Key Takeaway:
Influence Mapping provided the structural picture. The Oversight Kit Framework then turned those findings into a professional, auditable oversight deliverable.
