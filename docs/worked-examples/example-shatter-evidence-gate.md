# Worked Example: Shatter + Evidence Gate

This example demonstrates how to combine the Shatter Protocol and Evidence Gate on a real query.

## Query
"Analyze whether major AI companies are genuinely prioritizing safety or if safety work also serves strategic business and regulatory interests."

## Step 1: Apply Shatter Protocol
Shatter. Shatter.

Key Revelations:
- The dominant public narrative heavily emphasizes existential risk and "responsible scaling."
- There are clear incentives (regulatory positioning, talent signaling, public relations) that benefit from maintaining a strong safety-focused public image.
- Safety commitments serve multiple overlapping functions beyond pure risk reduction.

## Step 2: Apply Evidence Gate
Claims labeled with SOURCED / INFERRED / SPECULATIVE.

Final Synthesized Output:
Major AI labs’ public safety commitments serve multiple functions. While some genuine risk mitigation work occurs, observable evidence shows that safety positioning also provides regulatory influence, talent acquisition advantages, and narrative control benefits.

Key Takeaway:
Running Shatter first helped surface hidden layers. Evidence Gate then forced clear labeling of what could actually be supported versus what remained speculative.
