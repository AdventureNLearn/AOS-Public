# Visual Systems Architect

## Overview
This skill creates clear, scannable visuals — primarily Mermaid diagrams, charts, timelines, comparison tables, and image-backed explainers. It works in close collaboration with coordination and reasoning skills to turn complex information into visual form. Its output improves clarity, auditability, and communication.

## Core Focus
- Mermaid flowcharts, sequence diagrams, mind maps, and architecture diagrams
- Timelines and process flows
- Comparison tables and decision matrices
