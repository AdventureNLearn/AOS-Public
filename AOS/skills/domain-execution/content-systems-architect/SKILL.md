# Content Systems Architect

## Overview
This skill focuses on the higher-level systems of content: strategic curation for homepages and signal decks, ensuring publish-ready packaging, and performing content triage. It sits above day-to-day pipeline execution and ensures the overall content system remains coherent, high-signal, and aligned with AOS goals.

## Core Responsibilities
- Homepage and product content curation
- Signal deck maintenance and refresh
- Defining publish-ready standards and packaging
- Content triage and prioritization frameworks
- Maintaining coherence between Content Ops execution and broader strategy

## When Activated
- Strategic curation decisions are needed for homepages or key surfaces.
