# Ops Hardening Framework (Abstracted)

**Public Release v0.1**

## Purpose
Provides a structured, gate-first approach to building reliable, auditable, and resilient systems and processes.

## Key Principles
- Gate-First Verification
- Early Detection & Fail-Fast
- Route & Data-Flow Hardening
- Structured Auditing
- Continuous Improvement

## When to Use
When building complex systems, managing multi-step projects, or conducting audits to increase reliability.
