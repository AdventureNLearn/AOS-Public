# Sovereign Lens (Abstracted)

**Reasoning Overlay Framework**

## Purpose
Sovereign Lens is a structured reasoning overlay designed to improve depth, scope handling, and output quality on complex, high-stakes, or multi-domain tasks. It supports clearer analysis in environments where narrative pressure, institutional complexity, or hidden control layers are present.

## Core Concept (Civic Context)
Instead of relying on a single flat response, Sovereign Lens breaks complex work into scope definition, module routing, validation gates, and observable reasoning. This approach is particularly valuable for civic intelligence work involving policy, power structures, funding networks, or institutional behavior.

## Key Features
- Strong emphasis on defining scope before deep work begins.
- Use of validation gates to catch weak reasoning or unsupported claims.
- Support for parallel and sequential module activation.
- Designed to reduce hallucination and increase traceability.

## When to Use (Civic Use Cases)
- Multi-domain or cross-scope civic research and analysis
- High-stakes oversight or accountability investigations
- Tasks requiring clear boundaries and auditable reasoning
- Situations involving institutional or narrative complexity

## Integration
Can be used standalone or in combination with Shatter Protocol (as a Layer-0 pre-filter) and other verification primitives such as Evidence Gate.
