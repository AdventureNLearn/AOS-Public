# Influence & Organizational Mapping Framework (Abstracted)

**Public Release v0.1**

## Purpose
Provides a structured approach to mapping relationships, influence, funding flows, personnel movements, and organizational power structures.

## Key Components
- Entity Identification
- Relationship Mapping
- Influence Analysis
- Disclosure & Transparency Tracking
- Confidence Scoring
- Structured Output

## When to Use
For investigating organizational influence, mapping funding networks, analyzing coordinated messaging, or preparing background for oversight.
