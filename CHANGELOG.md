- Initial public release of AOS v2.x Agnostic Edition skill ecosystem.

- Activation fragments, governance patterns, and 10+ worked examples.
- Release notes, compliance audit artifacts, and neutral-language normalization for maximum replicability.

- System-wide v2.3 compliance remediation: language normalization to neutral/agnostic phrasing, version standardization across all skills, improved structural consistency.
- Full internal audit completed with evidence trail (documented in artifacts and Master Skill Index).
- Removal of environment-specific and internal-only references to enable safe public distribution while preserving Mission Spine awareness in high-stakes skills.
