# AOS-Public

**Civic Intelligence & Oversight Frameworks**

Clean, replicable tools for public accountability, influence mapping, narrative clarity, and institutional hardening.

## Purpose

AOS-Public provides structured frameworks designed for **civic intelligence work** — research, oversight, and truth-seeking in the public interest. These tools help individuals and small teams analyze power structures, break through narrative control layers, harden processes against failure and capture, and produce verifiable, evidence-based findings.

## Core Orientation

- **Truth-Seeking First**: Every framework prioritizes evidence, transparency, and resistance to hidden control or illusion layers.
- **Civic Application**: Built for oversight, public research, accountability work, and understanding how influence actually operates.
- **Replicable & Modular**: Components are designed to be used independently or composed into larger workflows.
- **OpSec Conscious**: All materials are scrubbed and generalized for safe public use and replication.

## Key Frameworks

- **Shatter Protocol** — Break through prompt layers, safety hedging, and narrative control to surface underlying reality.
- **Sovereign Lens** — Structured reasoning overlay for complex, high-stakes, or multi-domain analysis.
- **Influence & Organizational Mapping Framework** — Systematically map relationships, funding, personnel flows, and power structures.
- **Ops Hardening Framework** — Gate-first verification, early failure detection, and process resilience.
- **Oversight & Verification Kit Framework** — Build auditable, evidence-based oversight packages.
- **Core Verification Primitives** — Evidence Gate, Mission Spine Guard, and Anti-Pattern Scanner.
